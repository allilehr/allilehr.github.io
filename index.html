People are multifaceted. Shouldn't your style reflect that? I know that some days I want to dress like a total badass, hey I'm headed skydiving, yeah I'm jumping out of a fucking plane. Other days hipster grunge, anyone else hitting up that warehouse party in Brooklyn?! Back home in Annapolis, going out on the boat, prep me outttt. It's not even always the place you're headed, but the mood you're in. Some days I just want to brood and wear all black graphic tees, some days I want to smile and shine like the sun in all sorts of colors. The point is, rarely are people just one type of look. I wanted a brand that supports and represents that. We are all multifaceted. Don't feel stuck in one style because because you're scared you can't "pull off" that look. You can pull it off and you will rock it. Let us inspire you to try different looks for any venue, mood, and state of mind. Remember, you're a unique individual with the right to express yourself in many ways. Embrace every piece of your personality. You are strong and beautiful; you are a rock and a rose.
